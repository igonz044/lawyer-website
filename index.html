Our Practice Areas

EB-5 Investor Visas

For investors who invest $800K+ in a U.S. business and create jobs; leads to a Green Card.

E-2 Treaty Investor Visas

For citizens of treaty countries who invest in and run a U.S. business; renewable long-term.

L-1 Executive Transfers

For executives or key employees transferring from a foreign company to a U.S. office.

O-1 Visas

For individuals with extraordinary talent in fields like business, science, arts, or sports.
