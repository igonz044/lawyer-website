O-1 Visa (Individuals with Extraordinary Ability or Achievement)

Type: Non-immigrant visa
Ideal For: People who are exceptionally talented in fields like science, business, education, arts, sports, or entertainment.

Requirements

  • Must show extraordinary ability via awards, media recognition, original contributions, etc.
  • Requires a U.S. sponsor or agent.
  • Must work in the field of extraordinary ability.

Why High-Net-Worth Clients Like It

  • Ideal for celebrity entrepreneurs, founders, artists, athletes, influencers, scientists.
  • Fast processing.
  • No labor certification required.
