Our Practice Areas

Specialized legal guidance to navigate complex challenges.

Immigration Law

We provide comprehensive legal services for all immigration needs, guiding clients through the complexities of visa applications, green cards, and citizenship processes with clarity and care.

EB-5 Investor Visas

Our firm assists foreign investors in navigating the EB-5 visa program, facilitating investments that lead to permanent residency in the United States.

E-2 Treaty Investor Visas

We specialize in helping investors from treaty countries obtain E-2 visas, enabling them to establish and operate businesses in the U.S.

L-1 Executive Transfers

We support multinational companies in transferring key executives, managers, and specialized knowledge employees to the U.S. through the L-1 visa category.

O-1 Visas (Extraordinary Abilities)

Our firm has a successful track record of securing O-1 visas for individuals with extraordinary abilities in the sciences, arts, education, business, and athletics.
