L-1 Visa (Intra-Company Executive Transfer)

Type: Non-immigrant visa
Ideal For: Executives, managers, or employees with specialized knowledge who work at an overseas company and want to transfer to a U.S. branch, office, or affiliate.

Requirements

  • Must have worked at the foreign company for at least 1 year in the past 3 years.
  • The U.S. office must be a qualifying affiliate (subsidiary, parent, branch, etc.).
  • The applicant must be coming to work in a managerial, executive, or specialized role.

Benefits

  • Often used by business owners expanding to the U.S.
  • Can lead to a Green Card through the EB-1C category.
  • No minimum investment required.
