EB-5 Investor Visa (Green Card for Investors)

Type: Immigrant visa → leads to a Green Card
Ideal For: Foreign investors who want to permanently live in the U.S.

Requirements

  • Invest $800,000–$1,050,000 in a U.S. business.
  • Investment must create at least 10 full-time jobs for U.S. workers.
  • Investor must prove the funds are lawfully obtained.
  • Investment can be made in a Regional Center project (passive) or directly by starting/running a business.

Benefits

  • Direct path to U.S. permanent residency for them and their family.
  • No need to have a job offer or employer sponsor.

Case Study: EB-5 Visa – Green Card Through Investment

Client: International real estate investor from South Korea

Goal: Permanently move to the U.S. with his family

Solution: Invested $900,000 in a USCIS-approved Regional Center project

Outcome: Secured conditional Green Cards for himself, his spouse, and two children. He now lives in Southern California and manages U.S.-based investments.
